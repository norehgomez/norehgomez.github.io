TOURIST SPOTS IN THAILAND

Discover the most breathtaking destinations across Thailand, a land filled with rich culture, history, and adventure!

Wat Phra Chetuphon

Known as the Temple of the Reclining Buddha, this site has an impressive Buddha statue covered in gold leaf. The stunning murals depict Buddha’s teachings.

Chatuchak Weekend Market

This market in Bangkok has over 15,000 stalls across 35 acres. It offers street food, clothing, antiques, and local culture in one vibrant place.

Bangla Road

Located in Patong, Phuket, Bangla Road is vibrant at night with bars, street performances, and lively music. It’s a must-visit for nightlife lovers.

The Grand Palace

The Grand Palace served as the residence for Thai kings. Its stunning architecture includes the sacred Temple of the Emerald Buddha, a must-see site.
