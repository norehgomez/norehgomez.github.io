Marine Life Facts

  • Coral reefs are home to 25% of all marine species.
  • Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood.
  • Some fish can change their gender, like the clownfish.

Conservation Tips

  • Avoid single-use plastics to protect marine life.
  • Participate in beach clean-up drives.
  • Support organizations working to save the oceans.
